## Ad Copy Best Practices

### Headlines

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Include primary keyword
- Lead with benefit or value proposition
- Use numbers and specifics ("Save 50%", "14-Day Trial")
- Create urgency ("Limited Time", "Today Only")
- Ask questions ("Tired of [Problem]?")

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use all caps (except acronyms)
- Make unrealistic claims
- Use excessive punctuation (!!!)
- Violate platform policies
- Use clickbait

### Descriptions

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Expand on headline benefit
- Include social proof (reviews, customer count)
- Address objections (free shipping, returns)
- Use clear call-to-action
- Highlight unique selling points

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Repeat headline verbatim
- Use jargon or technical terms
- Forget call-to-action
- Ignore character limits
- Use generic copy

### Ad Extensions (Google Ads)

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Use all relevant extensions
- Keep sitelinks concise (25 characters)
- Update promotions regularly
- Use location extensions for local businesses
- Add structured snippets for features

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use duplicate sitelinks
- Forget to update expired promotions
- Use broken links
- Ignore mobile optimization

## A/B Testing Ideas

### Elements to Test

1. **Headlines:**
   - Benefit-focused vs. Feature-focused
   - Question vs. Statement
   - With/without numbers

2. **Descriptions:**
   - Short vs. Long
   - Social proof vs. No social proof
   - Urgency vs. No urgency

3. **CTAs:**
   - "Start Free Trial" vs. "Get Started"
   - "Shop Now" vs. "Buy Now"
   - "Learn More" vs. "Download Guide"

4. **Images/Videos:**
   - Product photos vs. Lifestyle images
   - Static vs. Carousel
   - Image vs. Video

5. **Offers:**
   - Percentage discount vs. Dollar amount
   - Free trial vs. Free demo
   - Free shipping vs. Discount

## Performance Benchmarks

### Google Ads (Search)

- **CTR (Click-Through Rate):** 3-5% (good), 5%+ (excellent)
- **Conversion Rate:** 2-5% (average), 5%+ (good)
- **Quality Score:** 7+ (aim for 8-10)
- **CPC (Cost Per Click):** Varies by industry ($1-$50+)

### Facebook Ads

- **CTR:** 0.9% (average), 2%+ (good)
- **CPC:** $0.50-$2.00 (average)
- **CPM (Cost Per 1000 Impressions):** $5-$15
- **Conversion Rate:** 1-3% (average), 3%+ (good)
