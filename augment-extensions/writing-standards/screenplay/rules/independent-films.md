# Independent Films

## Overview

Independent films prioritize artistic vision, character depth, and authentic storytelling over commercial formulas. These films often explore complex themes, experimental structures, and intimate human experiences with creative freedom.

## Defining Characteristics

### Budget and Production
**Typical Budget**: $100K - $20M
- Limited resources require creativity
- Smaller cast and crew
- Fewer locations
- Practical effects over CGI
- Longer shooting schedules relative to budget
- Festival circuit distribution

### Artistic Freedom
- Director/writer-driven vision
- Unconventional narratives
- Risk-taking and experimentation
- Personal stories
- Social commentary
- Authentic voices
