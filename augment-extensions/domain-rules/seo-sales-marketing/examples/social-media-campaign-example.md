# Social Media Campaign Example: Product Launch

## Campaign Overview

**Campaign Name**: "AI Analytics Platform Launch - Transform Your Data"  
**Duration**: 90 days (March 1 - May 31, 2026)  
**Platforms**: LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook  
**Budget**: $15,000 ($5,000 paid ads + $10,000 content creation)  
**Primary Goal**: Generate 500 MQLs and 100 free trial signups

---

## Campaign Strategy

### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Data analysts, business intelligence professionals (25-45 years)
- **Secondary**: C-suite executives, marketing managers
- **Industries**: SaaS, E-commerce, Finance, Healthcare
- **Pain Points**: Manual data analysis, siloed data, lack of actionable insights

### Key Messages
1. **Primary**: "Turn data chaos into actionable insights in minutes, not hours"
2. **Supporting**: "AI-powered analytics that speaks your language"
3. **CTA**: "Start Your Free 14-Day Trial"

### Content Pillars
1. **Educational** (40%): Tips, tutorials, best practices
2. **Product Features** (30%): Demos, use cases, benefits
3. **Social Proof** (20%): Customer stories, testimonials, case studies
4. **Engagement** (10%): Polls, questions, behind-the-scenes

## A/B Testing Plan

### Test 1: CTA Variations (Week 1-2)
**Variable**: Call-to-action text
**Version A**: "Start Your Free Trial"
**Version B**: "See It In Action - Free Trial"
**Metric**: Click-through rate
**Winner**: Version with higher CTR gets 100% traffic Week 3+

### Test 2: Visual Style (Week 3-4)
**Variable**: Image style
**Version A**: Product screenshots
**Version B**: Lifestyle images (people using product)
**Metric**: Engagement rate
**Winner**: Version with higher engagement continues

### Test 3: Post Length (Week 5-6)
**Variable**: Copy length
**Version A**: Short copy (100-150 characters)
**Version B**: Long copy (300-400 characters)
**Metric**: Engagement + click-through rate
**Winner**: Version with better combined metric
